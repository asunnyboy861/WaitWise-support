Getting Started

WaitWise helps you track and manage your healthcare appointment wait times. Here's how to get started:

  • Add an Appointment - Tap the + button on the Home screen to create a new appointment entry with your referral date, specialist type, and estimated wait time.
  • Track Progress - View real-time progress on your wait times with visual indicators showing how your actual wait compares to the estimated wait.
  • Set Reminders - Enable notifications to receive updates when your wait time approaches or exceeds the estimated duration.
  • View Statistics - Check the Statistics tab to see trends and insights across all your appointments.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I add a new appointment? - Tap the + button on the Home tab and fill in the appointment details including provider name, specialist type, referral date, and estimated wait time.
  • Can I edit an existing appointment? - Yes, tap on any appointment card to view its details, then tap the Edit button to make changes.
  • How are wait times calculated? - Wait times are calculated from your referral date. The progress ring shows your current wait relative to the estimated wait time you entered.
  • Is my data secure? - Yes, all your data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit any personal health information.
  • Can I export my data? - You can share individual appointment details using the Share button on the appointment detail screen.

Troubleshooting

  • Notifications not working - Make sure notifications are enabled in your device Settings > WaitWise > Notifications.
  • App not updating wait times - Wait times update automatically based on the current date and your referral date. Try closing and reopening the app.
  • Data not saving - Ensure you have sufficient storage space on your device. If the issue persists, try restarting the app.
